The Therapeutic Ultrasound and Modulation Laboratory lab, lead by Dr Sophie Morse, looks at how we can non-invasively modulate cellular activity, such as that of immune cells, to treat and delay the progression of disorders, including neurodegenerative diseases and tumours.

### The Morse lab aims to: 

* Uncover the level of control ultrasound technology can achieve in modulating cell activity
* Understand the mechanisms behind such modulation
* Investigate how this technology is best used to achieve therapeutic benefits as we age
